InMind – Age 14–16 | 32 Week Day-Level Dynamic
Content Plan
Daily Static Flow:
Context Warmup → Energy Check-In → Emotion Check-In → Body Map
Dynamic Flow:
DailyPrompt → Scenario → MicroFeedback → Reflection → Insight → Challenge → Completion
Weekly Psychological Structure:
Monday → Awareness
Tuesday → Recognition
Wednesday → Conflict
Thursday → Real-Life Mapping
Friday → Behaviour Action
Saturday → Mind Lab Reinforcement
Sunday → Weekly Snapshot
