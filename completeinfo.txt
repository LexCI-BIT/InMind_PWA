RESPONSE TIME CALCULATION RULE
response_time_ms = response_submit_timestamp - screen_render_timestamp
Meaning:
• 
• 
• 
very low response time may indicate impulsive/non-reflective behaviour
moderate response time indicates likely engagement
extremely high response time may indicate distraction/confusion
Recommended interpretation ranges:
Range
< 2 sec
Interpretation
likely rushed interaction
2–8 sec
healthy interaction
> 20 sec distraction/avoidance/confusion
IMPORTANT: These are behavioural indicators, NOT conclusions.
HESITATION TIME CALCULATION
hesitation_time_ms = first_interaction_timestamp - screen_render_timestamp
Meaning:
• 
• 
high hesitation may indicate uncertainty/emotional conflict
extremely low hesitation may indicate habitual tapping
